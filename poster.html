The Hmong people descent from China, Southeast Asia, most notably from Thailand, Vietnam, and Laos. As a civilization, Hmong people do not have
their own country. As of today, one of
the largest Hmong communities resides
in St. Paul, MN.

Hmong culture has slowly spread and grown with great influence in their cultural foods and traditional clothing throughout the Metro area. Every year Hmong organizations host Hmong New Years to celebrate its heritage where traditional Hmong clothing is worn as formal attire while attending.
